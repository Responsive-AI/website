How Responsive Works

Responsive is built on a foundation of trust and privacy. Because we know learning and growth best take place with the psychological safety of confidentiality, just like with an in-person coach.

Responsive integrates with your existing workflow tools—Gmail, Google Calendar, Slack, Microsoft Teams, and beyond—to bring the learning experience directly into your work environment.

You get personalized coaching based on your level, role, experience, and goals in addition to behavioral and neuroscience research and leading-edge people management practices.

Frequent feedback is crucial. Responsive requests quick and anonymous micro-feedback from you immediately after calendar events conclude, increasing frequency, accuracy, and convenience.

You receive targeted learning resources based on your current work, micro-feedback, and your personalized Responsive coaching plan.

You get relevant action learning opportunities linked to your calendar and workflow so you can practice what you learn directly in your work.

You’ll track your growth with Responsive’s dashboard analytics, benchmarking, and career builder. We are committed to your career growth, so Responsive has been designed so you can keep your account when you change companies.

Responsive’s community provides opportunities for you to learn from others, match with a peer mentor, ask questions, and get support from experts.

Frequent micro-feedback from team members is at the heart of Responsive’s learning model. Feedback, though, is never shared direcly and is always confidential. Our mission is for you and your team to learn and to succeed.

In addition to our mobile and desktop apps, Responsive integrates with your existing workflow tools—Gmail, Google Calendar, Slack, Microsoft Teams, and beyond—providing a streamlined coaching partner for you and your entire team.

Each team member gets personalized coaching based on your individual inputs, micro-feedback from team members, your role, and your growth and performance goals.

Responsive’s micro-feedback algorithms determine the 1-4 scaled questions most relevant for each team member to complete following calendar events—usually taking about 5-10 seconds total.

Each team member receives targeted learning resources based on the team’s activities, growth opportunities, team dynamics, behavioral data, and performance objectives.

Great teams aren’t just about great managers—everyone contributes to the team’s success. With Responsive, each team member is given relevant learning opportunities linked to your calendars and workflow.

With Responsive, each team member will have access to team dynamics dashboards designed to provide relevant, meaningful, and actionable data visualizations that show how each individual and the team are improving and comparing with other teams in your company and beyond.

Learning best takes place in community. Responsive connects you with a broad community of mentors, experts, and other teams that can provide support, guidance, and perspective.

Responsive is designed to be a confidential coaching platform for individuals and teams that want to perform at their best. Identifiable individual and team performance data are never shared with skip-level managers, HR, executive teams, or anyone else.

Responsive Analytics provides companies with valuable predictive analytics on organizational culture, targeted development recommendations, and opportunities for cultivating broad performance improvements.

When combined, both Responsive and Responsive Analytics give individuals, teams, and company leadership invaluable metrics, insights, and recommendations for scaling organization and business performance.

Human-Centered

Responsive is personalized to focus individuals and teams on crucial growth opportunities that sustain success. Utilizing frequent micro-feedback, neuroscience research, leading-edge management approaches, and machine learning, each team member receives unique learning opportunities relevant to the team’s success.

Privacy

Just like working one-on-one with a coach, confidentiality is crucial for the psychological safety needed so learning can flourish. With Responsive, feedback is used only as an input and is never shared directly. This way, individuals and teams can focus on learning and growth, not evaluations. Anonymized benchmarking helps you understand where you and your team are compared with others.

Agile & Adaptive

Responsive continuously adapts to provide teams with growth opportunities relevant to the team’s immediate needs by capturing input from team members, calendars, people changes, distributed teams, and more.

Scalable & Portable

Teams, jobs, and companies are scaling like never before. Responsive is designed to be your personalized coach from team to team, job to job, and company to company, so you can continue your development and growth throughout your career.

Organizational Insights

Responsive Analytics provides companies with crucially dynamic insights into organizational health, culture, and areas of opportunity while maintaining individual and team privacy so people can be focused on learning and team productivity. By capturing continuous micro-feedback from users, insights are much more relevant than an annual engagement survey. Responsive Analytics is based on actual experiences rather than perceptions.
